Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. They help websites remember certain information and work more smoothly.

How we use cookies

  • Preference cookie: We store your last search keyword so we can show it again below the search bar on your next visit for faster repeat searches.
  • Basic analytics: Our hosting or analytics tools may use cookies to measure traffic and detect errors.

We do not use cookies to create detailed advertising profiles or to track you across the web ourselves.

Third‑party cookies (ads)

We display ads from third‑party ad networks, currently Monetag. These partners may set their own cookies to show and measure ads and to protect against fraud.

Any data collected by them is handled under their own privacy and cookie policies.

How to manage cookies

  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • If you disable cookies, the site will still work, but your last search keyword will not be remembered.
